Before you apply...

Before applying with LaunchLab, make sure you are an Open Window student and that you have a personal online portfolio.
Not having a portfolio will get your application rejected no matter your design skills or experience.

This can be a custom portfolio site, or a link to a Cargo, Behance, Carbonmade, Dunked, etc. account.

Please note that the following are not accepted as portfolios:

  • Personal blogs
  • Dribbble accounts
  • Forrst accounts
  • PDF portfolios
  • Flash-only portfolios
  • Any password-protected portfolio

What If I'm Not Accepted?

If your application is not accepted, you can reapply any time. That being said, we review each application individually, so please only apply if you meet the requirements!

Originality Policy

The basic principles of the LaunchLab community include honesty, originality, and respect.
Do not copy other designers’ work. Understand that some similarity is to be expected.

Obvious concepts are open to be developed by all designers, for example:

  • using baseball for a sports shop logo
  • arranging the initials of a company.

    • However, designers must create their own unique implementations, and must not merely attempt to replicate the decisions made by designers who receive high star ratings.  Any concept presented in the brief can be developed by all designers. Unique and original concepts must be respected and only developed by the designer that introduced them. Should a dispute arise, please be polite and professional while the dispute is resolved. Failure to follow the rules will result in your LaunchLab account being suspended or possibly closed forever.
